September 1 2020
I’ve been figuring out ideas for project 4. The theme is based around the Internet and although countless words that hint at the Internet pop up, not a lot of them are appealing enough for me to base my entire project on. Nonetheless, I narrowed my ideas down to three main ones:
1. A website that educates Internet users on what cookies are and what they do. The website is interactive, educational, and simple to understand and use. This could raise Internet users’ awareness on what cookies are.
2. A physical guidebook introducing Paywave. The guidebook will cover everything one should know about Paywave and what it could mean for the future.
3. An app about QR codes. This app can scan any QR code and has a wide encyclopedic collection of QR codes from numerous sources. This app will teach users how to identify QR codes and how they work. .
